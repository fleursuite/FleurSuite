Features

Why FleurSuite?

We make utilities optimized for Windows. No web browser wrappers, no high RAM usage, just clean speeds.

⚡

Natively Built

Written in C# and WinUI 3. Boots in milliseconds and uses almost zero system memory.

💎

Acrylic & Mica

Stunning interface materials dynamically blurring behind your custom desktop wallpaper.

🔒

Local Privacy First

All document files remain stored directly on your computer. Offline by design, secure forever.

🌷

Custom Accents

Sync your workspace accent color with your system configuration instantly.
